About Me

I'm a third year computer science student at the Georgia Institute of Technology.
Currently, I'm a research intern for the Computing and Society Lab at Georgia Tech.
I'm researching data of cellular broadband access with the goal of increasing access
to the internet in rural and indigenous communities. It's really cool to be learning
about what places like this lab and companies such as Microsoft and Ookla are doing
to bridge the gap in these communities.

Outside of that, I'm learning HTML/CSS and JavaScript to build this React website.
I used Java to build this cool Clash of Clans themed Tower Defense game, and I made
this fun GBA game in C - both of which you can play below! (Work in progress.)

I am extremely passionate about continuing my learning and pursuing opportunities
that will allow me to expand on my skills. The goal of this website is to
demonstrate to you, how excited I am to do that.
